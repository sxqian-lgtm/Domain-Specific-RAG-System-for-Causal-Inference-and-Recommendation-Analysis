The provided synthetic CSV contains 15000 observations for 5000 users and includes user
level features together with pre and post indicators. Key columns include age, device type,
yearly subscription status, genre preference, a baseline engagement variable, treatment
assignment through rec_syst, a time index t, a post indicator, a major release context
flag, and the outcome minutes_viewed. This schema is well suited for a teaching case in
causal recommendation analysis because it combines behavioral covariates with a treatment
indicator and repeated timing information. It also gives the project a bridge between
business terminology and causal terminology. For example, rec_syst maps naturally to
treatment, while minutes_viewed is the outcome of interest. The dataset can support simple
DID calculations, regression adjustment, and discussion of confounding.
